Our Capabilities

Making wellness and growth a culture to all easier and smarter.

Creative Coterie is a Non Gorvernmental Organization with the aim of becoming a safe place for individuals and creatives, encouraging self-development and wellbeing through creativity and wellness travel to allow healthy living and growth as they impact lives and society with their knowledge, skills and creative abilities. Contributes to the achievement of SDG 3.

Our pograms

Live Grow Conversation

A safe space where we discuss anything and everything to encourage and promote healthy living, growth and wellbeing. We encourage each other to Live and Grow.

Quarter Growth

A quarterly recap of life and growth in the year to help track and achieve progress in your growth and goals.

Youth Hangout

An annual youth day hangout in August to commemorate the International Youth Day as we hangout to discuss important issues concerning the youth, play games, enjoy poetry and music as well as eat good food.

YESD Program

A Youth Empowerment and Skill Development Program designed to empower young people and to train and equip them with skills that will help them make impact and fetch income as they work with an organization or set up businesses for themselves.

See-site travels:

Wellness tour and hangouts designed to provide a safe space for individuals and creatives to hangout and experience therapy as they interact and engage in fun and therapeutic activities. We share, we discuss and we learn. Individual and small group (20 people max) tours and hangouts.

Beautiful

just the way you are: An outreach program designed for all genders to project self-acceptance and self-love, encouraging young people (adolescents and young adults) to accept and love themselves feeling confident in their bodies; their shape, size and skin color.
